Exclusively at TOQUO

Toquo is a high-energy Pan-Asian bar and restaurant located in Gokulpeth, Nagpur. Situated on the 8th and 9th floors of the West High Building, it offers a vibrant dining experience with a modern take on traditional Asian cuisine.

Classic Chilli Potato

Experience the perfect crunch with our golden-fried potato fingers, wok-tossed in a vibrant, tangy, and sweet chili sauce with crisp onions and bell peppers. A timeless appetizer that's irresistibly savory and spicy.

Crispy Vegetable Sushi

A delightful textural contrast in every bite. An assortment of fresh, crisp vegetables and creamy avocado, rolled in seasoned sushi rice and nori, then finished with a crunchy tempura coating. Served with a drizzle of our signature sauce for the perfect umami finish.

Crystal Vegetable Dimsum

Delicate, translucent wrappers envelop a finely minced medley of vibrant, seasoned vegetables. Gently steamed to perfection, these bite-sized dumplings offer a clean, flavorful, and wonderfully light experience. Served with a classic dipping sauce to complement the fresh filling.

Vegetable Fried Rice

A quintessential comfort classic, perfected in our high-heat wok. Fluffy, long-grain rice is stir-fried with a colorful mix of crisp, garden-fresh vegetables and seasoned with a light and savory soy-based sauce. Simple, aromatic, and deeply satisfying.

Stir Fried Noodles

Springy noodles wok-tossed over a roaring flame to capture that signature smoky 'wok hei' aroma. This classic dish features a crunchy selection of fresh vegetables, all brought together in a rich, savory sauce that perfectly coats every single strand.

Vegetable Mala Sauce

Brace yourself for an electrifying flavor journey. A curated selection of exotic, crisp vegetables are stir-fried in our authentic Mala sauce, famous for its unique numbing (ma) and spicy (la) sensation derived from Sichuan peppercorns and fiery chilies. A bold and addictive dish for the adventurous palate.

Toquo Tres Leches

A Japanese-inspired twist on the beloved Latin classic. Our incredibly light and airy sponge cake is soaked in a signature blend of three milks, creating a dessert that is unbelievably moist and decadent. Finished with a delicate, lightly sweetened cream topping for a melt-in-your-mouth experience that's both rich and refreshing.
